About Our Product

COIN PAPAD: Papad is a thin, crisp disc-shaped Indian food typically based on a seasoned dough made from urad flour, fried or cooked with dry heat and are typically served as an accompaniment to a meal in India. Apart from the traditional Indian Urad and Moong Papad , we specialize in papads of Rice ,Maize , corn and potato. Our papads (size of upto 2.5 inches) are such that it can be eaten anytime as meal accompaniments, supper snacks

Heeng Mixture

Heeng mixture namkeen, is made from crispy fried gram flour, peanuts, dal, nukti, etc. seasoned with flavorful spices. The quantity of Heeng or asafoetida is more than the other spices, giving the Heeng mixture namkeen a distinct and elevated taste experience.

Ujjani Sev

Ujjaini sev is a crunchy, thin, and light snack from the Malwa region of Madhya Pradesh, Ujjaini sev is a traditional food and is one of the most widely consumed sev variants. It's a popular snack with Indian food lovers around the world.
