About Our Product

COIN PAPAD: Papad is a thin, crisp disc-shaped Indian food typically based on a seasoned dough made from urad flour, fried or cooked with dry heat and are typically served as an accompaniment to a meal in India. Apart from the traditional Indian Urad and Moong Papad , we specialize in papads of Rice ,Maize , corn and potato. Our papads (size of upto 2.5 inches) are such that it can be eaten anytime as meal accompaniments, supper snacks
